Jenny Knuth's Future

10 Things About the Future

  • I want to stamp out things that say, "If YES press OK".
  • The correct use of the hyphen/dash is very important to me.
  • I love languages but get tired of squeezing meaning out of linear sentences.
  • For me, design is multi-layered communication: not just linguistic, but also visual communication and interaction design (choreography).
  • I want to learn everything I can about the new frontiers of data visualization.
  • I am beyond excited about the web's ability to not only display data, but to also make it interactive (and beautiful).
  • I want to take something like that geo-biography on the other page and make it into an interactive chart.
  • I want to take things like this interactive bar chart of sea ice extent that I made in D3.js and link it to this animated GIF map of sea ice extent.
  • After gSchool, I want to help make the web more useful and more beautiful. I want to take my curiosity and add to it expertise and a career.
  • I would like to work at a place like Foraker labs or eSource and help their clients visualize their data and provide the best user experience possible.
